Green Rivet

Mission

Ensure permanence of settled populations in developing nations
by increasing resilience to detrimental effects of climate change
through development of open source designs, systems, and tools.

---

Founder's comment: A major cause for climate change is the pollution from developed nations' unsustainable practices.
The cost of localised growth has had a global price tag, which calls for funneling the benefits of innovation towards those who would otherwise suffer the most.

Note: The mission of the initiative explicitly excludes preventative measures and is focused on adaptation.

Name

The green in the initiative's name symbolises life and sustainability and rivet communicates determination and permanence.
Together they stand for an adaptive force binding the land and its inhabitants.
